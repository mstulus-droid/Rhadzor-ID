Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Kaleindo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our calendar application.

By using Kaleindo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address (if you create an account)
  • Calendar Data: Events, notes, and reminders you create
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and unique device identifiers

Important: All your calendar data is stored locally on your device by default. We do not upload your personal events or notes to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Kaleindo service
  • Sending holiday notifications and event reminders
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Responding to your support requests
  • Analyzing usage patterns to develop new features

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your calendar data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Request information about data we hold about you

Children's Privacy

Kaleindo is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
